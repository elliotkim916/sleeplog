Keep track of how much you sleep because your rest is vital

Sleep is an important indicator of overall health and well-being. We spend up to one-third of our lives asleep, and the overall state of our "sleep-health" remains an essential question throughout our lifespan.

Sleep needs are individual and change as you age. Within a day, newborns need a total of 14-17 hours, infants need a total of 12-15 hours, and school age children need 9-11 hours. Teenagers need 8-10 hours, and young adults and above need 7-9 hours.

Based on your age, are you getting enough sleep? This app is a simple and effective tool for keeping track of how many hours you slept, noting how you felt when you woke up, and saving any other details you'd like to add about your rest.
